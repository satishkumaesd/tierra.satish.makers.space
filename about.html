About Us

Home About us

We advise. We Teach. We Educate.

Tierra’s Makers Space “Make Your Ideas Come True ” we are one of the pioneers in industrial IoT and provide a better teaching manner with different technical skills by real-time application-based experiments. Tierra develops self-learning kits for beginners, researchers, students, corporate, institutes and enthusiasts to help in technological research & development. We develop innovative and solitary project ideas as well as electronics-based systems & machines that can be used to automate various operations in industries. Our organization builds some systems to automate work from various sectors, including agriculture, manufacturing, defense, and Retail sector.

We Create a workspace for practical exposure to technology, experimentation, fabrication, and prototyping. Space is intended to support product startups, foster innovative and efficient engineering projects; and nurture a community of makers within Tierra’s Maker’s Space.

Maker’s Lab aims to imbibe and inculcate the Entrepreneurial vision and Core values of Students. Makers Lab is fully loaded with 3D Printers & Scanners, IoT & Electronics.
