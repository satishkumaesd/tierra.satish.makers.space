Makers Lab

Our Makers Lab?

We provide a space for people those who want to make their idea come true. But don't know where to start and How to start. Here in our lab we have all the required material for making your ideas come true

Electronic Components

Electronic Components include: Development Boards Sensors, Wireless Modules, Acctuators, IC's, Etc

3D Printer

We have a FDM 3D Printer with a maximum print size of 220cm*220cm*250cm(l*b*h).

PCB CNC Machine

We have a PCB routing machine with the accuracy of 0.01mm which can route 90cm*130cm pcb very accutately. Along with that we have all the required mateial for prepairing larger pcb's also.

Tools

All Cutting, Gringing, drilling ,etc tools are available.
