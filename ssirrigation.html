Smart Irrigation System

What is Smart Irrigation System??

From this workshop you will get knowledge about smart irrigation systems. The Smart irrigation System has wide scope to automate the complete irrigation system. Here we are building a IoT based Irrigation System using ESP8266 NodeMCU Module Moisture sensor and DHT11 Sensor. It will not only automatically provide the water based on the moisture level in the soil but also send the Data to ThingSpeak Server to keep track of the land condition. The System will consist of a water pump which will be used to sprinkle water on the land depending upon the land environmental condition such as Moisture, Temperature and Humidity.
