RFID Railway Crossing System

What is RFID Railway Crossing System??

At present, India has the world’s largest railway network which has a manual gate control system. Over hundreds of railways running on track every day. It is definitely impossible to stop the running train immediately in some critical situation or emergency arises. Train accidents having serious drawbacks in terms of loss of human life, injury, damage to Railway property. This model uses automatic control or operation of the gate and reduces the human labors. The concept of the model is to control the railway gate using RFID(Unique Codes). The main Objective of this idea is to avoid an unwanted accident, happening at the railway level crossing due to less awareness of drivers and poor work of gate keeper and also time saving.
